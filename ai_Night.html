Hackathon information

  • Category: Hackathon
  • Issuing organization: Association for Scientific Research and Innovation in Computer Science
  • Issue date: 1-2 April, 2023

Description:

"My team and I won 1st Prize in the AI Night Competition, hosted by ARSII and Novation City. The challenge, presented by N A DEV, involved using AI to detect lung cancer from x-ray images. We used Unet, Alexnet, and VGG models in Python, comparing results from Image-net and Kaggle data. This experience was immensely rewarding, and I'm grateful for my talented teammates' hard work and dedication."
