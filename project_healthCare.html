Project information

  • Category: Artificial Intelligence
  • date: December, 2022 - April 2023

Description:

"Presenting Health&Care, a groundbreaking AI-powered medical project that marks a significant milestone in our Machine Learning journey. Health&Care is an all-encompassing medical solution, designed with a vision for the AI-driven future of healthcare. It unites the detection of seven diseases under a single platform, using the extraordinary capabilities of AI. While the results of this project are currently experimental, they hold the potential to be a transformative force in the years to come. The primary advantage of Health&Care is its ability to deliver test results instantly, right to your home, with just a few clicks.

Let's delve into the details of each disease detection:

COVID-19 Detection: I developed a custom-made CNN architecture for this purpose, achieving an impressive 93% accuracy rate.


Brain Tumor Detection: Leveraging VGG-16 for feature extraction and a custom-made CNN, I achieved a remarkable 99% accuracy rate (based on a test of 10 images).


Breast Cancer Detection: For this use case, I employed Random Forest, delivering a strong 91% accuracy rate.


Alzheimer's Disease Detection: Trained CNN architecture was the key, achieving a 73% accuracy rate.


Diabetes Detection: Using Random Forest, I achieved a 66% accuracy rate for this particular use case.


Pneumonia Detection: A custom CNN architecture helped me achieving an 83% accuracy rate.


Heart Disease Detection: I utilized XGBoost, resulting in an 86% accuracy rate.


Health&Care isn't just a project; it's a vision for the future of healthcare, where AI empowers individuals to access their health status conveniently and promptly from the comfort of their homes. Together, we're taking a significant step towards this exciting future of AI-driven healthcare."
